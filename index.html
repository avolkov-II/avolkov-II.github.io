About

I'm a systems engineer with 8+ years of experience building low-latency trading infrastructure. My work spans order matching engines, market data feeds, and latency profiling tools — all optimised down to the cache line.

I believe performance is a feature, not an afterthought. Every microsecond matters when you're competing for liquidity, which is why I focus on lock-free data structures, kernel bypass (DPKC), and careful memory layout.

Currently I work at Aether Innovations in London, where I design next-generation exchange simulators and lead the migration of legacy C++ systems to Rust.

Philosophy

  • Measure, don't guess — profile before you optimise
  • Own the stack — understand the hardware your code runs on
  • Simplicity scales — lock-free doesn't mean complex
  • Ship fast, ship safe — strong typing prevents bugs at compile time
